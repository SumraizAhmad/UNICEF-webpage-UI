Why UNICEF?

UNICEF has helped save more children’s lives than any other humanitarian organization – 90 million since 1990. UNICEF is the world’s largest provider of ready-to-use therapeutic food, a high-protein paste that can bring a malnourished child back to health within weeks. UNICEF immunizes nearly 40% of the world’s children.
